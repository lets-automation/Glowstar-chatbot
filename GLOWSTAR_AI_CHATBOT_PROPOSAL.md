# GlowStar AI Data Assistant

## Proposal & Commercial Options

**Prepared for:** Glow Star (Natural Diamonds)

**Scope:** An AI assistant integrated into Glow Star's existing ERP — ask any
business question about your data in plain English and get an instant, accurate
answer, with one-click export to Excel, PDF, or charts. No reports to build, no
SQL, no new software for your team to learn.

---

## What you get

A bespoke AI assistant that plugs into your current AasthaErp database (read-only
— it never changes your data) and turns everyday questions into instant answers:

### 1. Ask anything, in plain language

- Type a question the way you'd ask a colleague — *"How many packets are on
  jangad?"*, *"Show RD stones above 1 carat by clarity"* — and get the answer in
  seconds
- Understands diamond & jewellery terminology out of the box: shape, colour,
  clarity, fluorescence codes, jangad, packets, purity, and your coded columns
- Handles typos and broken English, so anyone on the team can use it
- Remembers the conversation, so follow-up questions just work
- Every answer is backed by the exact data it pulled — no invented numbers

### 2. Instant reports & exports

- Turn any answer into a clean **Excel** sheet, **PDF**, or **chart** with one click
- No more waiting on someone to build a custom report
- Self-explanatory output your team can share immediately

### 3. Safe by design

- **100% read-only** — the assistant can only *read* your ERP, never insert,
  update, or delete anything
- Built-in guardrails block unsafe queries; every question is logged
- System and login tables are hidden from the assistant

Everything runs **inside your existing ERP infrastructure** — delivered as API
endpoints + a simple chat screen. No separate server, hosting, or domain cost.

## Running costs 

Runs on your existing ERP infrastructure — **no server, hosting, or domain
charges from us.** The only running input is the AI (Claude) usage itself.

### Claude AI API charges (paid by you, directly to the AI provider)

The assistant is powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI). Each question your team
asks uses a small amount of AI processing, billed **per question, directly to
your own Claude account** — pass-through, with **no markup from us**. You pay
only for what you actually use; there is no fixed monthly fee.

- **~₹7.50 per question** (current Claude rates)
- A built-in optimisation (response caching) can cut this by roughly **half** to
  **~₹4 per question** — switched on before go-live at no extra charge

**Estimated monthly Claude API cost by usage:**

| Usage level | Questions / month | Standard | With caching |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light (~25/day) | 500 | ~₹3,800 | ~₹1,900 |
| Medium (~50/day) | 1,000 | ~₹7,650 | ~₹3,800 |
| Standard (~100/day) | 2,000 | ~₹15,300 | ~₹7,600 |
| Heavy (~250/day) | 5,000 | ~₹38,250 | ~₹19,100 |

> The AI bill scales exactly with how much your team uses the assistant — never a
> fixed cost. Every question is logged, so usage is fully transparent and you can
> check the exact figure at any time.

**No other running costs.** Your database, server, and ERP are already yours; the
assistant adds nothing to them.

## Scope notes

**Included:** read-only natural-language Q&A over your AasthaErp database; chat
screen; API endpoints into your existing ERP; Excel / PDF / chart export;
session memory; safety guardrails; business glossary; deployment & handover.

**Not included:** any feature that *writes to* or modifies your ERP data;
building new ERP modules; replacing your existing ERP/CRM; a full custom
analytics dashboard (the chat screen + exports cover this; can be quoted
separately); fuzzy/meaning-based search (optional add-on).
